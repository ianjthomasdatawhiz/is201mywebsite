Board Games

  • Wingspan

    Wingspan is an engine building game where you place unique birds on your player mat and gain their abilities. We love it in my family because of how different it is each game and how beautiful the art is.

  • Hadara

    Hadara is a strategy game where you build up resources. What I like about this game is how everyone plays at the same time, so little time is wasted waiting for other players.

  • Scythe

    Scythe is a competitve board game that pushes you to think about your resource managment and planning with every turn. With amazing artwork and complex mechanics, this game is my favorite. Below is a video of how to play.
